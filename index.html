Our Story

What started as a small cart serving chilled sugarcane juice in Lavington has grown into a full farm to glass operation with a purpose. We partner closely with smallholder farmers across Kenya, paying fair weekly rates and providing consistent demand that supports their livelihoods.

The Sugarcane Man is reimagining the journey of sugarcane from farm to glass. By building an ethical, transparent supply chain, every cane pressed becomes a story of empowerment, sustainability, and fair trade.

Our mission is bigger than juice. We run a youth employment and skills-training program that equips young people with hands-on experience in production, customer service, delivery logistics, business operations, and entrepreneurship. Every bottle you enjoy directly fuels job creation, confidence building, and community growth.

From farm, to press, to bottle, we keep it fresh, natural, and impactful.
